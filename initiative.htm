Food CPR - Compose. Plant. Reduce

Details Content
Goal Manage food waste effectively and reduce the amount of waste generated to landfills for a more
sustainable environment. Sunway Group is committed to advancing the United Nation's Sustainable
Development Goals, one of which is halving Malaysia's food waste by 2030 -
a pledge made by the local government
Date 20.09.2019 ~ 06.10.2019
Time 1000 ~ 2200
Venue Sunway Pyramid, Malaysia
Organizer Sunway Pyramid
Fee Free
How to
Participate		 During the event, shoppers can take part in potting workshops, which include seed planting and pot
decorating. A compost dispenser will also be available for compost collection, which can be used for
gardening and planting. Additionally, shoppers can redeem various seeds by downloading the
Sunway Pyramid Mobile App

Saving the Earth, One Pack at A Time with Milo

Details Content
Goal Encourage shoppers to recycle used beverage cartons through the CAREton Project
Date 09.10.2020 ~ 29.11.2020
Time ~
Venue LG2 Orange Wing, MILO UHT CAREton Project Pop Up Booth, Sunway Pyramid, Malaysia
Organizer Sunway Pyramid, MILO UHT, Tetra Pak
Fee Free
How to
Participate		 Shoppers can recycle their used beverage cartons by following these steps:
1. Rinse out the used cartons to keep away pests and odour
2. Push the straw back into the empty pack or re-attach the cap of the cartons
3. Flatten the cartons by doing the Flip-Flap-Flat method
4. Drop off the flattened cartons at the Recycling Zone located at CP2, Sunway Pyramid
